# Epic: Aircraft Fleet Management

**Product:** Fighter Plane 3

**Priority:** Medium

**Status:** Generated

## Description

**Why does this epic matter?**

- **Business Problem**: The current lack of a centralized system for managing aircraft profiles and statuses leads to inefficiencies in mission planning and fleet management.
- **Customer Pain Point**: Mission planners need real-time visibility into aircraft availability and conditions to effectively allocate resources for missions.
- **Expected ROI**: By optimizing fleet management, we anticipate a reduction in downtime and increased mission success rates, leading to more efficient air force operations.
- **Alignment with Company Strategy**: This epic directly supports our vision to streamline operations and enhance readiness, aligning with the military's goals for modernization and efficiency.

**Connection to Initiative Goals**: This epic contributes to the overarching initiative of providing a unified platform that consolidates various operational components, ensuring that air force operations are more integrated and efficient.
