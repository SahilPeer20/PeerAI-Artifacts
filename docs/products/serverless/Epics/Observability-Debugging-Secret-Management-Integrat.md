# Epic: Observability, Debugging & Secret Management Integrations

**Product:** Serverless

**Priority:** Medium

**Status:** Generated

## Description

**Why does this epic matter?**

Observability and Secret Management are essential for secure, reliable, and scalable production operations. Integrated logs, metrics, traces, and secret referencing reduce time to resolution for incidents, improve security posture, and enable compliance for enterprise teams. These capabilities are major differentiators in enterprise adoption and retention, directly impacting platform NPS and long-term ROI.

**Connection to Initiative Goals**:
Delivers on the promise of "production-grade" serverless—secure, observable, and governed.
